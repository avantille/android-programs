This project shows an example of incorporating external image recognition software created by IQ Engines
 
The first window prompts to register/log in. You must therefore register first if you want to progress further to
access the application's menu.

Click on the Challenges tab, choose your options from drop down lists (time  and difficulty) and click on
'Set your treasure hunt' to generate list of random items. click on any of those item and camera will
start. You must take a photo of appropriate item in order to connect with IQ Engines. You also need 
internet connection on your mobile device to be on.
